pgammafit Documentation

Introduction

pgammafit uses the Poisson-gamma model to perform a multi-bin, multi-source Bayesian fit, based on the paper

``Bayesian analysis of multisource data,'' Pushpalatha C. Bhat, Harrison B. Prosper, Scott S. Snyder, Phys.Lett. B407 (1997) 73-78, DOI: 10.1016/S0370-2693(97)00723-5, FERMILAB-PUB-96-397

which may be found at

https://inspirehep.net/record/441459
