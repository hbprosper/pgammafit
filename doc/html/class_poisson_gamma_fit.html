Detailed Description

Fit Poisson/gamma model to data. The data consists of a sequence of counts (i.e., a histogram), presumed to arise from a mixture of sources, say , , and events. The goal is to find the yield of each source, given sequences of counts that describe the ``shapes" of the sources. In other words, given a data histogram and histograms , one for each source , we wish to find the yield of each. This class solves the problem using a Bayesian approach [1].
